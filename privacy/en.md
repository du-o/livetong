# LiveTong Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

LiveTong values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the LiveTong app handles user data.

## 1. Information We Collect

The current version of LiveTong does not require account registration or sign-in. We do not actively collect sensitive personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, contacts, precise location, photos, microphone, or camera data.

The app retrieves publicly available concert information from our servers, including but not limited to event titles, artist names, event dates, venues, cities, ticketing platforms, ticketing stages, images, and source links.

## 2. Information Stored Locally

When you follow an artist or city, add an event to your wishlist, or use the Planner feature, LiveTong stores necessary event text information, follow preferences, and planning information locally on your device. This information is used to display followed events, your wishlist, and your Planner.

This locally stored information is not uploaded to LiveTong servers by default. You may unfollow artists or cities, remove wishlist items, or delete relevant records in the app. When you uninstall the app, locally stored information is generally deleted together with the app data.

## 3. Images and Network Requests

Event images are generally loaded from our servers or public sources. To improve loading performance, the app may temporarily cache images locally on your device.

When you actively choose “Save Share Image,” the app requests permission to save the generated share image to your system photo library. LiveTong does not read, scan, or upload other content in your photo library.

When the app requests data from LiveTong servers, our servers may automatically record necessary technical logs, such as IP address, request time, request path, app version, device operating system version, API response status, and error information. We use this information only for service operation, security protection, abnormal access detection, and troubleshooting.

To provide server hosting, network, and image storage services, relevant technical data may be processed by cloud service providers engaged by us. We take reasonable measures to require them to process such information only for service provision and security purposes.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use relevant information to:

- Display Japanese concert information;
- Support filtering by city, date, artist, ticketing stage, and ticketing platform;
- Support the Wishlist and Planner features;
- Improve data loading speed and service stability;
- Diagnose errors, abnormal access, or service failures.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The current version of LiveTong does not include advertising SDKs and does not conduct cross-app or cross-website tracking.

Ticket links may redirect you to third-party ticketing platforms or official venue websites. The processing of data after you visit a third-party website is governed by that third party’s privacy policy. LiveTong does not control how third-party websites process data.

LiveTong only provides event information aggregation and links to third-party websites. We do not provide ticket sales, ticket purchasing on behalf of users, ticket issuance, payment processing, refunds or changes, or ticket resale services. Ticket eligibility, ticketing stages, prices, event times, and venue arrangements are subject to the real-time information published by the event organizer or ticketing platform.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or use personal data for targeted advertising or cross-app or cross-website tracking.

To the extent necessary for server hosting, network transmission, image storage, troubleshooting, and security protection, we may engage technical service providers to process necessary technical data. Except where required by applicable laws or necessary to protect the service and user safety, we do not provide personal data to other third parties.

## 7. Data Deletion

You may remove wishlist records within the app. Once removed, the relevant event will be removed from your local wishlist and Planner.

If you uninstall the app, locally stored wishlist data and cached images are generally deleted together with the app data.

## 8. Children's Privacy

LiveTong is intended for general users seeking concert information and is not primarily directed to children. We do not actively collect children’s personal information.

## 9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy based on product features, data processing practices, or legal requirements. Updated versions will be made available through the GitHub page or relevant in-app entry points.

This Privacy Policy applies to the LiveTong app in the regions where it is made available. Because servers, networks, or technical service providers may be located outside your region, necessary technical data may be transmitted to and processed in different regions. We will take reasonable security measures in accordance with applicable laws.
